The 'cowsay' esh plug-in
-----------------------
This plugin implements 'cowsay'
Prings the users input in a cow's speech bubble.
usage: cowsay <any amount of input>

sample output:
esh> cowsay mooo
Valid Input
  ---
/    \
| moo |
\    /
  ---
       \     ^__^
        \    (oo)\_______
             (__)\       )\/\
                 ||----w |
                 ||     ||
