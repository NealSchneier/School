Integer to Letter Grader Converter.

This is our plugin which allows you to enter your grade as an integer and it 
returns the letter (+/-) representation.


How to run:  Invoke our converter by entering What's_my_grade? followed by an
integer.  Check out the examples below.

Sample
................
What's_my_grade? 95 
Congragulations, you got an A

What's_my_grade? 101
You are such an over-achiever
